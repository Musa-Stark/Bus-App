Terms & Conditions

  1. Tickets and fares: Bus tickets will be sold based on the fare system set by the Transporter. Any changes to the fare structure will be communicated through the BusApp or other communication channels. It is the customer’s responsibility to check and confirm the fare before booking.

  3. Cancellation and refunds:
    a- Cancellation: Cancellations are subject to approval by the bus operator, meaning that each cancellation request will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Approval is not guaranteed, and decisions will be made according to the operator’s policies and criteria.
    How it works:
    When a passenger requests a cancellation, the request is forwarded to the bus operator for review. The bus operator can approve or deny the request based on their cancellation policy and the communication between the operator and the passenger.
    b- Refund: Once the cancellation request is approved, it takes up to 10 working days to process the refund. The refund amount may be subject to deductions as per the bus operator’s refund policy. Please refer to the end of this page for detailed refund policies of all bus operators.
